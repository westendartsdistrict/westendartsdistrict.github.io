Having postponed Animate Dance Festival to Fall 2021, we have redirected our energies this year into the creation of a site specific piece for the Animate Shorts series. “Breathe” is a reflection on the crazy times we have all been living through, a celebration of our ability to support each other; to move, breathe, and dance together despite it all. Given the current spike in cases, it looks like we will not be able to perform live for you in 2020, so we've put together a few clips from rehearsals to give you taster. We look forward to sharing the piece with you in person in early 2021.
