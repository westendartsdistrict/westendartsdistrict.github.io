Doolittle Raid 

Wings of adventure turn into wings of war, transforming Alameda into the “Gateway of the Pacific” and a Navy town that tripled its population. Local boy Jimmy Doolittle was back in uniform ready to serve his country. 

Maze Daiko: Janet Koike (Artistic Director), bean (Tina Blaine), Kathryn Cabunoc, Elaine Fong

Writer: Ed Holmes

Actor: Robert Paine 

This performance is an excerpt from Island City Waterways: Uprooted 

Premiering May 21st & 22nd at Alameda Point. More info at: islandcitywaterways.org
