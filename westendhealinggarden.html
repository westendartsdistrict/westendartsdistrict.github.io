The Healing Garden is a testament to how the vision of a few strong community leaders can mobilise the goodwill of many to produce something truly inspirational.  A collaboration between West End Arts District and the West Alameda Business Association, the vision started with the exhibition of BLM artwork on the plywood boards donated by local businesses after the protests, expanded to the design and creation of a beautiful garden space, and will soon host an Alfresco Dining Park. 

THANK YOU TO ALL THOSE WHO HAVE VOLUNTEERED TIME, MATERIALS, SUPPORT AND FUNDS.  

The Healing Garden plays host to the Alfresco Dining Park.  For more info about dining options and events  keep an eye on Facebook.

The Healing Garden will need to be watered once a week.  We're hoping that community members will help us by SIGNING UP on the schedule for the weekly watering.
