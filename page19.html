West End Mercantile is a weekly art & craft market in the West End Arts and Entertainment District featuring handmade wares from local makers. Held every Saturday on the sidewalks of Webster Street, the event is FREE and open to the entire community.   
  
Many local artists and businesses were hit hard by cancelled events and retail closures during Covid-19. The West End Mercantile hopes to provide an evolutionary opportunity for those artists and businesses to connect with the community. Masks and social distancing are required. Hand sanitizer as well as contactless payment options are available, and shoppers are asked to only touch what they plan to buy to keep everyone safe.
