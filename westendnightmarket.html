The West End Night Market is an extension of the popular 2nd FridayArt Walk. With live bands, street vendors and food trucks grouped together in the vacant Taylor lot at the corner of Taylor @ Webster, Alameda,  the night market brings the street to life and gathers young and old for some good tunes and local crafts.
