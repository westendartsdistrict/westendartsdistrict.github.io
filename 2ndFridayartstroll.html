Beginning in February, the West Alameda Business Association will kick off a fun-filled community event in support of our local businesses to help create a vibrant atmosphere for the West End community. You’re invited to an evening under the stars in the Al Fresco Dining Park filled with local artisans and vendors, food trucks, lawn games, spoken word, poetry, acoustic music, community theater, paint & sips and more! Join us every month for our 2nd Friday Art Stroll featuring local businesses curating artists and shows within their establishments from 5-9pm through December. This event is sponsored by the West End Arts District. For more information visit: https://www.facebook.com/2ndFridayArtStrollAlameda
