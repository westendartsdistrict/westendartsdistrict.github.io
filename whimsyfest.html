April 2016 –Whimsy Fest was a celebration of art, culture, and creativity all in an undiscovered corner of Alameda Point. The event featured the unveiling of four new murals, roaving brass bands, fire breathing art cars, acrobats dangling from their feet, and culminated in the world’s shortest parade.
