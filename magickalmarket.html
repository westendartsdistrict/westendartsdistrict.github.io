​Magickal Market brings together unique vendors, local artists, oracle readings, and workshops. While the shelter-in-place is in effect, Feathered Outlaw is offering a revised version of the free Magickal Market! We’ve curated some fantastic artisans and local vendors that will feature their magickal wares. Discover jewelry, apparel, ethically-sourced taxidermy, occult literature, crystals, glassware, candles, local honey, incense, Tarot reading, tinctures and more!
