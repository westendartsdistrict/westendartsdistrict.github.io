Welcome to the official Website of West End Arts District.

For your convenience, two types of limited information are gathered from visitors to our Website: anonymous usage information, and the personal information provided by those who subscribe to our newsletter. Details about what information we gather and how it is used is presented below.

Anonymous Information.

As a visitor, you are welcome to visit and browse the West End Arts District website as often as you like without logging in, and no personally identifiable information about your visit will be collected. Although our Web servers track usage information for these visits, it is on an entirely anonymous basis. This usage information is general data about how visitors use our Website, which pages are most frequently visited, which websites most frequently refer visitors to our site, and how long patrons spend on our site.

Our servers track your usage by logging your IP address. This usage information is aggregated and used only to monitor broad usage trends, to evaluate the effectiveness of our Website’s design, to determine what customers do and do not use on the Website, and to improve the Website’s overall usability.

We may perform, or have third parties perform, analyses of visitors’ behavior, and may share aggregated data with sponsors and advertisers, again on an entirely anonymous basis.

Personal Information

Personal information is any data related to the personal identity of a visitor to our Website. A customer’s name, address, phone number, credit card number, e-mail address, and purchase history are all examples of personal information collected. This data is encrypted by companies West End Arts District employs to perform functions on our behalf, such as processing credit card payments, fulfilling ticket orders, sending regular and electronic mail, removing repetitive information from customer lists, or analyzing data. Such companies and individuals have access only to the personal information necessary for performing their functions, but they are prohibited from using it for other purposes.

We do not require visitors to provide any personal information in order to browse our Website or to obtain general information on our Website. We do, however, require Website users to provide limited personal information in order to subscribe to our email list, purchase tickets, or make contributions. In these cases, the only personal information collected is that which a visitor specifically provides when registering.

West End Arts District uses personal information from subscribers to add to or update our email lists for our digital newsletter, and special offers.

West End Arts District does not share or sell your personal information with other not-for-profit organizations and/or arts sponsors.

Links from Our Site

West End Arts District website includes links to other websites. We do not control the privacy policies of other destination sites. Once you leave West End Arts District servers (you can determine where you are by checking the URL in the location bar on your browser), use of any information you provide is governed by the privacy policy of the operator of the site you are visiting.
