COLLEGE OF ALAMEDA 

RETURN TO PROGRAM PAGE


Director and Choreographer: Danny Nguyen

Performers: Nash Ang, Nan Busse, Danny Nguyen, Anna Rodas, Caroline Valentino, Gintara Wiguna, Cynthia Yu, Hannah Zhu, Tracey Enskip, Andy Nguyen, and Mira Isabella Seagrave

PART 1: "Essence of Vietnam"

This piece is about bringing most of the Vietnamese Culture into the dance through elements such as: Costumes, Stage Props, and Music.

PART 2:  "Let's All Hope"

Throughout the past 2 years, many have suffered and many have died. This piece is dedicated to the pain felt by our friends and family in Vietnam. Dancing to a poem (written by: Doan, Huong Vi and played by Hugo Nguyen, read by Michael Masumoto), the dancers dream and pray for a world of “no more tears” where life is “full of love and there is no hatred at last.” This “dream for humanity” takes a very real turn when, to music by Shigeru Umebayashi (George’s Waltz), the dancers personify the struggle to live……
