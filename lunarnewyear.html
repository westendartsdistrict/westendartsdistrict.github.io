Presented in collaboration with the West Alameda Business Association and the Toi Shan Family Association,  the Chinese New Year Celebration (also known as the Lunar New Year) is a new tradition on Webster Street. Festivities are at the corner of Webster and Haight right by the Webster Farmers Market and include Lion dances, Chinese Traditional Dance, Tai Zhi performance and kids arts and crafts activities.
