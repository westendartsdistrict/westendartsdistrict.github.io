Alameda choreographer Tara Pilbrow teamed up with local photographer Maurice Ramirez, and dancers Hannah Westbrook, and Stacey Yuen to explore Webster Street for fun art opportunities.  In the build up to our first major public art commission, the Webster Gateway Mural, we're highlighting how much art in the community can brighten our lives, and help us to see our everyday surroundings in a new light.
